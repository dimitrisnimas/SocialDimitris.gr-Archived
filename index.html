About Us

We Are Social Dimitris

We are your trusted partner in the ever-evolving landscape of digital marketing. Established in 2018 at Trikala, we have steadily grown to become a digital marketing agency with a strong presence in cities like Athens, Ioannina, Thessaloniki, Kozani, and beyond.

Over the past six years, Social Dimitris has had the privilege of serving a diverse clientele, with more than 15 satisfied customers who have witnessed the transformative power of our digital marketing solutions.

At Social Dimitris, we specialize in navigating the dynamic world of social media, leveraging the full potential of platforms like Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, and more. Whether you're looking to boost your brand presence, engage with your audience, or drive sales, we are here for you. We are more than a digital marketing agency; we are your dedicated partners in success. At Social Dimitris, we don't just create campaigns; we build lasting connections.
